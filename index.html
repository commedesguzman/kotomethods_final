Each KOTO Kit contains two essential components tailored to each user's needs: the Travel Kit and Disposal Needle Box. Each box was made to ensure convenience and safety during travel, addressing users' mobility and storage concerns. With these specialized features, users can confidently manage their medication with comfort and ease.
