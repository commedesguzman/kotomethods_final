What's Inside My KOTO Kit?

The KOTO Kit contains the different contents, which includes; Travel Kit, Disposal Needle Box, Insulin Pen and Needles, KOTO Sticker, KOTO Acrylic Charm, and User Manual.

How To Dispose My Needles

The Travel Kit is provided for users who need their insulin on-the-go, and to ensure that their insulin is stored safely.

Using The Disposal Box

The Disposal Needle Box ensures that each user's used needles are disposed of safely after each cycle.
